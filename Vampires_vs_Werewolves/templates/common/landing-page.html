Welcome to the epic battle of "Vampire Vs Werewolves"!

Choose your side and join the eternal struggle between these immortal creatures. In this thrilling multiplayer game, you'll take on the role of a vampire or a werewolf and embark on an unforgettable adventure in a dark and mysterious world.

Unleash your supernatural powers and test your strategic skills as you fight for dominance. As a vampire, harness the night's power to mesmerize your enemies and drain their life force. If you're a werewolf, embrace the fury of the full moon, transforming into a formidable beast with unmatched strength and speed.

Explore an immersive land teeming with ancient ruins, eerie forests, and haunting castles. Discover hidden treasures and rare artifacts that will enhance your abilities and aid you in your quest for supremacy.

The fate of these two mythical races lies in your hands. Will you bring eternal night as a vampire or howl under the moon as a werewolf? The battle for supremacy awaits!

Attacking Rules:

  • Players can initiate attacks against other players.
  • Each player can attack a specific player up to 10 times per day.
  • After reaching the daily attack limit, players cannot attack the same player until the next day.

Hiding Rules:

  • Players can choose to hide, making themselves untargetable by attacks.
  • While a player is hiding:
    • They cannot initiate or receive attacks.
    • They cannot work.
  • Players can come out of hiding whenever they choose.

Working Rules:

  • Players can choose to work, earning rewards based on their level and chosen occupation.
  • While a player is working:
    • They cannot initiate or receive attacks.
    • They cannot hide.

Healing Rules:

  • Players have the option to heal themselves to regain lost health points.
  • Healing may take time or require items, and players can only heal up to their maximum health.

Leveling Up Rules:

  • Players can earn experience points (XP) through various actions, such as winning battles or completing tasks.
  • Gaining a certain amount of XP will result in the player leveling up.
  • Upon leveling up, a player's stats may improve, and they may unlock new abilities or items.

Potions and Bonuses Rules:

  • Players can use potions and other items to gain temporary bonuses in battles or other activities.
  • These bonuses have a limited duration and may confer advantages such as increased power, defense, or speed.

Gold and Currency Rules:

  • Players earn gold by winning battles, completing tasks, or engaging in other activities.
  • Gold can be used to purchase items, equipment, and other in-game resources.

Fair Play Rules:

  • Players are expected to engage in fair play and follow the game's rules.
  • Cheating, exploiting, or abusing game mechanics is strictly prohibited.

Communication Rules:

  • Respectful and appropriate communication is expected between players.
  • Harassment, bullying, or any form of offensive behavior will not be tolerated.

  • The game's rules and mechanics may be updated or modified over time to enhance gameplay and balance.
